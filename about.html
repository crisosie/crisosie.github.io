About me!

My name is Cristian Soler Sierra and I am a Spanish Multimedia engineer highly interested in creating tools for animation and videogame artists. I am currently finishing my Master's Degree on Computer Graphics from the URJC at Madrid.

In 2017 I started working as a Technical Director for Barreira Arte y Diseño, SL. an educational centre that develops every year an animation short film. Later on I started working for Bikes AIE on the movie "Bikes, The Movie" I therefore have quite some experience developing simple and intuitive tools with MEL, Python and PyQt.

I am a very creative person with many artistic interests such as photography, classical music and origami. I studied violin for 11 years and graduated from the Ciclo de Enseñanza Profesional - Especialidad Violín (Professional Music Studies - Violin Specialty) at Quart de Poblet's Mestre Molins conservatory.

I love learning new languages, from which I am fluent to different levels in 4 (Spanish, English, Valencian and French) and have basic notions of 2 others (German and Mandarin).

I have worked with C, C++, Java, html5 and javascript, css, Python and MEL. I have recently studied CUDA programming, modern OpenGL programming and implemented advanced rendering and simulation algorithms.

I hope you enjoy my portfolio which includes many of my university courseworks I did both at Universidad de Valencia, de Montfort University and Universidad Rey Juan Carlos.
