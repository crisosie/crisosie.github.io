About me

My name is Cristian Soler Sierra and I am a Spanish Multimedia engineer highly interested in creating tools for animation and videogame artists. I finished my Master's Degree on Computer Graphics at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos of Madrid (2019-2020). I studied Multimedia Engineering at Universidad de Valencia(2013-2018) and benefitted from an Erasmus Program with De Montfort Univeristy at Leicester, UK (2016-2017).

I've been working as a Lighting TD at Skydance Animation Madrid since April 2020 for three of their projects: Blush, Luck and Spellbound. For the first year at Skydance I was in charge of fixing general bugs for all departments. Currently my work is focused on the Shading, Lighting and Compo Departments where I polish their publishers, importers and main tools usually working on improving their UIs to make them more intuitive to use.
